*   **Smoke Signals:** Used for military warnings.

Ultimately, if the Romans had smartphones, they would have used them to achieve what their empire fundamentally needed: **unprecedented speed, perfect information control, and flawless coordination** to maintain a massive, sprawling, and often volatile civilization.
